Hey everyone!

I'm Demi and I'm a digital artist from Manchester, England with a love for art and games.

I love to combine them both by recreating items found within my favourite games,

turning them into Twitch emotes and badges.

I really enjoy working on such a small canvas as it is rather challenging

and requires me to push myself as an artist.

I have been creating art my whole life and have also studied it throughout college and university.
